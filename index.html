SPOTPOINT

Interactive Geography Game

Welcome to SpotPoint, a free online game built on Google Maps to test your knowledge of world geography. Guess countries, their flags, and cities directly on the map.

Select a continent, choose a mode (country name, flag, or city), and try to find the correct answer. SpotPoint helps you learn geography while having fun.

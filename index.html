SPOTPOINT

Interactive Geography Game

Welcome to SpotPoint, a free online game built on Google Maps to test your knowledge of world geography. Guess the countries, their flags, and their cities directly on the map.

Select a continent, choose your game mode (country name, flag, or city), and try to find the correct answer. SpotPoint is the perfect way to learn geography while having fun.
